    Tips and Tricks

    My baking tips are

    practical. efficient. resourceful. adaptable. empowering.

    Simple Swaps for Eggs

    If you're running low on eggs, substitute with ingredients like applesauce, mashed bananas, or yogurt. These alternatives maintain moisture and binding properties in your baked goods.

    DIY Buttermilk

    No buttermilk? No problem! Create a quick substitute by adding one tablespoon of vinegar or lemon juice to a cup of milk. Let it sit for a few minutes until it curdles, and use it as you would regular buttermilk.

    One-Bowl Wonders

    Opt for recipes that require minimal equipment and cleanup. One-bowl recipes not only save time but also reduce the number of utensils you need, making the baking process more efficient.

    Gluten-Free Flour Alternatives

    For gluten-free baking, experiment with alternatives like almond flour, coconut flour, or a gluten-free flour blend. Keep in mind that the texture may vary, so adjust the quantity accordingly.

    Oil for Butter

    In a pinch, replace butter with vegetable oil or even coconut oil. This substitution works well in various recipes, especially when you're short on dairy products.

    Creative Spice Substitutes

    Don't have a specific spice on hand? Improvise with alternatives! For instance, cinnamon can often be replaced with nutmeg or allspice, depending on what's available in your pantry.

    Instant Coffee Flavor Boost

    Enhance the richness of chocolate desserts by adding a teaspoon of instant coffee to the batter. This trick intensifies the chocolate flavor without the need for extra ingredients.

    Customizable Mix-Ins

    If you lack muffin liners, cut parchment paper into squares and press them into the muffin tin. This makeshift liner will prevent sticking and make cleanup a breeze.

    Microwave Magic

    When conventional baking is a challenge, turn to microwave-friendly recipes. Mug cakes, brownies, and cookies can be prepared quickly with minimal equipment, perfect for a dorm room or shared kitchen space.
